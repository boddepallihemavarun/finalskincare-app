✦ The Process

How AURA works

From scan to solution in under 30 seconds — powered by our proprietary AI model trained on 200,000+ dermatology images.

01

Capture

Allow camera access and position your face in the AURA oval frame. Our system auto-detects alignment.

02

AI Analysis

Our HuggingFace-powered model, fine-tuned on clinical dermatology data, analyzes skin texture, tone, and patterns in real-time.

03

Diagnosis

You receive a detailed report: condition name, severity, confidence score, and a full breakdown saved to your history.

04

Your Plan

Get personalized home remedies, product suggestions, and instant access to book a certified dermatologist if needed.
